# Phase 2 · CYCLE 1 — GOLD Reviews Frankincense & Myrrh

## Gold Reviews Frankincense

**Factual accuracy of Frankincense's synthesis**: LARGELY CORRECT.
- The three-movement structure (Destruction → Construction → Self-Critique) accurately reflects the developmentalist reading
- The five master questions are genuinely the central problems
- The contemporary relevance section is well-aimed

**One correction**: Frankincense states "Frege's Sinn vs Bedeutung" as a recurrence of the universals problem. This is imprecise — Frege's distinction is about MEANING and REFERENCE, not about universals per se. The closer Fregean parallel is his concept of "object" vs "concept" (Begriff). Minor but worth noting.

**Assessment**: Frankincense provides the interpretive framework that Gold cannot. Its value is in CONNECTING the discrete facts into a coherent narrative of philosophical development.

## Gold Reviews Myrrh

**Factual accuracy of Myrrh's risk map**: HIGH.
- Risk 1 (Socratic Paradox self-refutation): Legitimate philosophical point. The paradox has been discussed by Vlastos (1991) and Penner (1992). The "ironist" reading resolves it but at a cost.
- Risk 2 (Third Man never repaired): ACCURATE. This is the consensus view in analytic metaphysics.
- Risk 5 (Immortality arguments all invalid): ACCURATE from a modern logical perspective. The arguments are historically important but formally weak.
- Risk 6 (Third horn for Euthyphro): VALID — the divine nature theory (Aquinas) is a real third option. Myrrh correctly notes this.

**One challenge to Myrrh**: Risk 8 (Dialogue form makes Plato unfalsifiable) is slightly overstated. Scholars DO attribute views to Plato based on: (a) which character speaks the most and is unrefuted, (b) cross-references with Aristotle's reports of "Plato's" views, (c) the Seventh Letter (if genuine). The dialogue form is a challenge but not an absolute barrier.

**Assessment**: Myrrh is the strongest Gift. Its risk analysis identifies real, unresolved problems — not strawmen.
